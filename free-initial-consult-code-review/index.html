Free Initial Consult

Let's start a conversation

We can do an initial consult - either over the phone or in our office - to help us better understand your business and what you’re trying to achieve. From experience, we know how important it is to find the right fit - both that we fit you and you fit us!

What we’ll need from you

  • Get to understand your project
  • Your experiences and progress so far
  • A rough road map or big picture plan going forward

What we’ll provide back

  • Possible build approaches
  • Estimates of cost and time frame
  • Where we think you’ll face technical risk
  • Build vs buy

If you have some existing code that you’d like us to review, we’ll need to get access to your code repository (typically Github, Gitlab or Bitbucket) or a zipped version.

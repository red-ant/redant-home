        <h2>What is a Digital Product?</h2>

        <p class="lead">Digital Products are websites or apps that provide some form of utility to users. These allow people to “do” stuff. Unlike a static site, which might be used for marketing a product or service, these sites <strong>are themselves</strong> the product or service. They help users  learn new things, store information, or interact with others. Digital Products tend to be fairly dynamic, customising the experience and content based on that user. More on <a href="/digital-products/defining-a-digital-product/">defining a digital product.</a></p>

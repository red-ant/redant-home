  <h3>Project Description</h3>
The Fish Market is a learning application that aims to develop innovation, enterprise and creativity in young school children. This colourful and engaging solution explores commodity trading while encouraging entrepreneurial thinking -a marine Monopoly for little munchkins!

This was developed in conjunction with <a href="http://savv-e.com.au/">Savv-e</a> for The Le@rning Federation. This game won the highly commended <a href="http://www.aimia.com.au/home/awards/aimia-awards/hall-of-fame/11th-aimia-awards/11th-awards-finalists" target="_blank">Best Children's</a> award from <a href="http://www.aimia.com.au" target="_blank">AIMIA</a>.

  <ul>
    <li>An <strong>engaging and highly visual game</strong></li>
    <li>Promotes many different mathematical lessons</li>
    <li>Engages <strong>multiple learning styles</strong></li>
  </ul>

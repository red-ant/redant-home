  <h3>Project Description</h3>

  <p>U by Kotex is a leading femcare brand, with three main products – tampons, pads and liners. The consumer target market is broadly all women – but strategically focuses on teenage girls who are seeking information and exploring different brands.</p>
  <p>We've worked on a number of different sites for the UBK brand over several years. This current version reflects new packaging, as well as incorporating new content and features around the new age target.</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Website supports the brand strength and talks openly and honestly to teenage girls</li>
    <li>A sophisticated sampling system provides info and samples to teachers and girls</li>
  </ul>

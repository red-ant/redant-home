About Red Ant

We’re a cross functional technical agency of Developers, Digital Program Managers and Designers in Sydney, Australia.

We create custom digital platforms and apps in Open Source software - we specialise in Ruby on Rails, Angular and React.

We’ve been creating digital websites and products for over 22 years and in that time have worked on a wide variety of projects. Along the way, we’ve picked up quite a bit of know how and experience.

Core skills

Digital Product Design and Digital Road Mapping

Many of our clients have an idea for their digital product but need help to refine their dreams into actionable project plans. We’ll help you understand how to take your ideas and problems and create something truly unique. We can help you define your development goals, organise them into a logical sequence and determine how long it will take. We can help you work out how many resources are needed and what budget you’ll need and when.

Technical Development

Our team of developers are experienced in building complex Platforms and Digital Products. From Minimum Viable Products (MVPs) to complex e-Commerce Platforms our team is ready to build your stable and tested code base. We specialise in Open Source technologies such as Angular and Ruby on Rails. Using Open Source development allows you to build more features faster and develop a unique product that suits your business systems and outpaces competitors.

User Interface and User Experience Design

Whether it’s a compelling online customer experience or an easy to use interface for admin teams, our team of designers and front end developers create user experiences that simply work.

Ecommerce

We create Software as a Service (Saas) platforms that use data in powerful ways to deliver true customer value. We integrate simple and complex payment systems depending on your customer and business needs.
